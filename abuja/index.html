Every step gets us closer to a polio-free world

Finally, the plane descended into the Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport, which is named after Nigeria’s first President.

In Abuja, the capital city, we met with local Rotarians to discuss the polio action plan. Polio is a crippling and potentially fatal infectious disease caused by a virus that invades the brain and spinal chord, which many don’t seem to realize is still a threat to children in parts of the world.
